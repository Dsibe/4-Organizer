Release 2.4: Statistics page

A new version of 4-Organizer is ready!

The most important change in this version is the new "Statistics" page.
On this page, you can find your personal statistics about sorting and cleaning.

Available information:
• total size of sorted files;
• total amount of sorted files;
• time saved for you while sorting;
• time saved for you instead of searching;
• top destination folder by frequency;
• top destination folder by size;
• top extensions by frequency;
• top extensions by total size;
• amount of conflicts resolved;
• total size of rubbish files deleted;
• amount of rubbish files deleted;
• amount of empty folders deleted.

Other changes in this version include:
• fixed bug, when UI didn't load correctly in certain systems;
• fixed bug, when scan sometimes didn't start;
• minor improvements in UI;
• minor bug fixes;
• improved stability.
